Why buy an air fryer?

Air fryers use rapidly circulating hot air to cook food instead of deep frying it in oil. Here are the key benefits:

  • Uses significantly less oil than traditional frying
  • Produces crispy food with a tender interior
  • Cooks faster than many conventional ovens
  • Easy to clean with dishwasher-safe baskets on many models
  • Great for fries, vegetables, chicken, seafood, and reheating leftovers

How to choose the best air fryer

1. Capacity

  • 2–4 L: 1–2 people
  • 4–6 L: Small families
  • 6–8 L: Families of 4–6
  • Dual Basket: Meal prep and large households

2. Wattage

Higher wattage generally means faster preheating and cooking. Look for models with 1400+ watts for best performance.

3. Preset cooking modes

Useful presets include: French fries, chicken, steak, fish, vegetables, reheat, and bake. More presets mean more cooking versatility.

4. Easy cleaning

Look for removable, non-stick baskets and dishwasher-safe parts. This makes cleanup quick and convenient.

5. Safety features

  • Auto shut-off
  • Cool-touch handles
  • Overheat protection

Common mistakes to avoid when buying air fryers

  • Buying a capacity that's too small for your household
  • Ignoring available counter space
  • Choosing only based on price
  • Overlooking warranty and customer support
  • Forgetting to check noise levels and ease of cleaning

Are air fryers worth buying?

For many households, yes. If you enjoy crispy foods, want to reduce oil usage, and need faster cooking for busy schedules, an air fryer can be a practical kitchen upgrade.

Air fryers are especially valuable if you frequently cook fried foods, want to eat healthier, or have limited time for meal preparation.

Frequently asked questions about air fryers

Which is the best air fryer overall?
The Ninja Air Fryer Max XL is a top choice because it offers excellent cooking performance, ease of use, and consistent results.

Is an air fryer healthier than deep frying?
Generally, yes. Air frying typically requires much less oil than deep frying while still producing crispy food.

What size air fryer should I buy?
2–4 L for one or two people, 4–6 L for small families, and 6 L or more for larger households.

Can I cook frozen food in an air fryer?
Yes. Foods like fries, nuggets, vegetables, and snacks cook well directly from frozen without thawing.

Is an air fryer easy to clean?
Many modern air fryers have removable, non-stick, dishwasher-safe baskets, making cleanup simple and quick.
